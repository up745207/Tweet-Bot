About

Staying Safe Online

TweetBot was made as part of a disseration project for the University Of Portsmouth, the scope of the project was a research piece that looked into the effects of political propaganda in modern age.
Ben Nimmo is a digital forensic, has some fantastic work here, which was the inspiration for the project.

How it Works

The site currently is hosted locally of a Raspberry Pi and is coded in HTML, CSS & Python. The site connects to both the Twitter developer API and Google Vision to analyse twitter accounts for unhumanlike characteristics.
